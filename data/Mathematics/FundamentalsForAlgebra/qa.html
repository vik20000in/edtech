Review Questions - Questions and Answers

Q: Tick ( ) the appropriate answer: (i) What is the value of x in the equation 2x + 5 = 15? (a) 4 (b) 5 (c) 6 (d) 7

The correct answer is (b) 5. Solving the equation: 2x + 5 = 15, subtract 5 from both sides to get 2x = 10, then divide by 2 to get x = 5.

Q: Tick ( ) the appropriate answer: (ii) Which of the following is an algebraic expression? (a) 5 + 3 (b) 7x - 2 (c) 10 ÷ 2 (d) 8 × 4

The correct answer is (b) 7x - 2. An algebraic expression contains variables, constants, and operations.

Q: Tick ( ) the appropriate answer: (iii) What is the coefficient of x in the expression 4x + 7? (a) 4 (b) 7 (c) x (d) None of these

The correct answer is (a) 4. The coefficient is the number multiplied by the variable.

Q: Tick ( ) the appropriate answer: (iv) Which of the following is a variable? (a) 5 (b) x (c) 10 (d) 3

The correct answer is (b) x. A variable is a symbol that represents an unknown value.

Q: Tick ( ) the appropriate answer: (v) What is the value of 3x when x = 4? (a) 7 (b) 10 (c) 12 (d) 15

The correct answer is (c) 12. Substituting x = 4 into 3x gives 3 × 4 = 12.
