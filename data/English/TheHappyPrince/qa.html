Review Questions - Questions and Answers

Q1: Who are the main characters in the poem "The Nightingale & the Glow Worm"?

The main characters are the Nightingale and the Glow Worm.

Q2: What does the Nightingale do in the poem?

The Nightingale sings beautifully and spreads joy with its melodious voice.

Q3: How does the Glow Worm respond to the Nightingale?

The Glow Worm responds politely, explaining that it also has a purpose and brings light to the darkness.

Q4: What is the main message of the poem?

The poem teaches us to respect the talents and contributions of others, as everyone has their own unique value.

Q5: Why does the Nightingale decide not to eat the Glow Worm?

The Nightingale realizes that the Glow Worm also has an important role and decides to respect its life and talent.

Q6: What qualities do the Nightingale and the Glow Worm show in the poem?

The Nightingale shows kindness and understanding, while the Glow Worm shows wisdom and humility.
